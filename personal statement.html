"A good personal statement could help you knock the door of your dream dental school."

Personal Statement is ...

  • Your voice and idea
  • A way to let dental school to know who you actually are

Essential components(2)

  • Theme

    a unifying idea, image or motif, repeated or developed throughout the statement

    Potential Themes
    1. Who are you
    2. Who/What has influence you to become who you are today
    3. The road traveled
    4. Why dentistry

  • Demonstrate

    your claims about your skills, abilities, interests, and/or personal characteristics

    For example:

    1. Special experiences(internships, community outreach, mission trips, research, etc.)
    2. Challenges overcome to reach your goal of becoming a health professional.
    3. How have those experiences influenced you?
    4. What special talents or leadership skills do you have?

All of the things above, just help you to brainstorm idea for your personal statement.After getting the idea, you will need to organize your idea into a well-written essay.
