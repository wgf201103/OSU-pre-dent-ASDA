About Us

We are the largest ASDA Chapter in the nation

Bi-weekly meetings on Wednesdays throughout the Fall and Spring Semesters

Meetings and events are not mandatory, but are geared towards helping you become a competitive dental school applicant and continuing to build a pre-dental community at Ohio State. It is up to you to make the most of these opportunities!

$20 annual dues

The fees will help us to provide awesome pre-dental events.It is not too much.
