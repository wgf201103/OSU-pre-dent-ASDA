DAT is not the only factor that decide whether you could get into dental school; However a good DAT score will help you get into better school and make up the shortcoming in your application. There are many DAT study plan, they are all from SDN. Note: Please do not focus on the score that the SDNer got too much. You could get either lower or higher score. Some peole use the method from a person who has score 22 AA on DAT but got 26 AA. There are also students use strategy from Ali(founder of DAT bootcamp, whose score is 25 AA), but got 21 AA. There is no best study plan. They are all excellent. Please choose the one that fit you best.
